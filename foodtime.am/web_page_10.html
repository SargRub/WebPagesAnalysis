Welcome to “Food Time” A Restaurant

Food Time was founded in 2018 on March 1.

In the city of Gyumri, Food Time is not the first fast food establishment, but thanks to its hard work it has become the fisrt and now Food Time is loved not only by residents of Gyumri, but also by our guests from different countries.
